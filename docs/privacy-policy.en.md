# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-19

This privacy policy applies to the `Chess Opening Trainer` app.

## 1. What data the app collects

In its current version, the app does not collect personal user data.

The app does not require:

- account creation,
- sign-in,
- email address,
- phone number,
- payment details.

## 2. Data stored locally

The app may store the following data locally on the device:

- app settings,
- selected openings,
- current training session state,
- interface preferences.

This data stays on the user's device and is not sent to the developer.

## 3. Data sharing

In its current version, the app:

- does not sell user data,
- does not share data with advertisers,
- does not send data to data brokers,
- does not use advertising or analytics SDKs.

## 4. Chess engine

The app uses the local Stockfish chess engine running on the user's device. Position analysis and engine moves are processed locally.

## 5. Permissions and internet access

The app may include the technical `INTERNET` permission, but in the current Android app flow it does not require user accounts or an external backend server for the core training and play features.

## 6. Children

The app is not specifically directed to children, but it does not contain adult-only content.

## 7. Changes to this policy

If future versions begin to collect data, use analytics, crash reporting, or external services, this policy will be updated accordingly.
